## ğŸ¯ Game Features

### Enemy Types
- **Basic Enemies** (Red): Standard speed, 1 health, 10 points
- **Fast Enemies** (Purple): High speed, 1 health, 20 points
- **Tank Enemies** (Orange): Slow speed, 3 health, 50 points

### Scoring System
- Destroy enemies to earn points
- Build up combos by destroying enemies quickly
- Every 5 enemies in a combo increases your score multiplier by 0.5x
- Combo resets if you take damage or stop shooting for too long

### Progression
- Level up every 500 points
- Each level increases enemy speed and spawn rate
- Game becomes progressively more challenging

### Lives System
- Start with 3 lives (hearts)
- Lose a life when an enemy hits you or reaches the bottom
- Game over when all lives are lost

### High Score
- Your high score is automatically saved in your browser
- Try to beat your personal best!
